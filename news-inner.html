Tkiyet Um Ali launches its Adha campaign under the slogan: “Your Udhiyeh Reaches Families in Jordan and Gaza”
17 Jul 2024

Tkiyet Um Ali has launched the 2024 Adha Campaign under the slogan "Your Udhiyah Reaches Families in Jordan and Gaza", aiming to provide Adahi meat to families in need in Jordan and Gaza Strip. Tkiyet Um Ali accepts Udhiyah donations based on the fatwa from the Jordanian General Iftaa Department.

The campaign highlights the importance of continuously supporting families in need throughout the year and assisting the people of Gaza under the current circumstances. Tkiyet Um Ali receives Udhiyah from donors & performs the Adahi on their behalf according to the religious specifications for Udhiyah. The Adahi are carried out in Australia and Jordan. The cost of Udhiyah in Jordan is 170 JOD and in Australia 120 JOD, which includes slaughtering, cutting, shipping, and storage for distribution to 22,000 families in need in 12 governorates and to families in Gaza Strip.

Tkiyet Um Ali simplifies the Udhiyah process through its website tua.jo, the TUA mobile app, e-wallets, shopping centers, or by calling 064900900. Donors can track the performance of their Udhiyah through the website and the app.
